L0 · Discovery

Marketing site — funnels users into registration. No business logic.

ehb-landing

Next.js · public

Marketing pages. No auth. Outbound links to ehb-main register and login flows. Funnel only.

L1 · Identity

Single sign-on. Every sub-platform exchanges an ehb_token for its own JWT here.

ehb-main

NestJS · MongoDB · port 5000

Stores only ehb_user_id, email, full_name. Issues short-lived tokens. Email is the cross-platform join key.

L2 · Trust

Verification engine + ongoing credential refills. Issues the SQ level every sub-platform consumes.

ehb-pss

PSS · port 3001

Personal Security Score. CNIC / NADRA / OTP / liveness / address / regulator lookups. Async — emits HMAC-signed webhooks back to the calling platform with the SQ decision.

CRB

Credential Refill Bench

Schedules per-industry refill cycles (license, certs, hygiene, CPD). Reminders, exams, QR-anchored certificates, auto-downgrade on missed refills.

L3 · Skills & Jobs

Canonical profile per (platform, role). The trust anchor service platforms render to end users.

ehb-jps

NestJS · port 3003

One JPS profile per (platform, role). Drives the public Owner card on every listing. Also: job postings, AI matching, contracts, salary disbursement, designation progression.

L4 · Services

Consumer-facing storefronts. Each consumes identity, trust, and skill from L1–L3.

ehb-gosellr

live · port 3002 (api) · 4002 (web)

SQ-verified marketplace. Roles: buyer, seller, rider. WebSocket-driven order tracking. Sellers must connect a JPS profile before listing.

HPS

planned · health

Hospital / doctor booking. PMDC license verification through PSS. Patient records owned by HPS.

OLS

planned · learning

Schools, tutoring, certifications. HEC + exam-board verification through PSS.

WMS

planned · workforce

Employer ↔ worker scheduling and payroll. Heavy JPS integration.

OBS

planned · business

Digital banking, SMB tooling. Heavy PSS KYB integration.

L5 · Governance

Only body authorised to slash trust. Receives complaints, disputes, fraud, AML from every service.

DMO

Decentralized Management & Oversight

7-tier escalation queue. AI auto-decisions for low risk; human officers for medium/high. Outputs: SQ downgrade, account freeze, ban, refund order, regional kill-switch. Immutable audit log.

L6 · Support & Distribution

The physical-world layer plus the internal cockpit.

Franchise System

regional

Physical verification visits, regional dispute resolution, revenue share on every order/contract/booking in their territory. Themselves SQ-gated.

Admin Panel

internal cockpit

Auto-extends per industry. Officer queue, CRUD viewers, permission matrix, audit trail, KPI dashboards.

The Universal Trust Pipeline

Every action in EHB — buying, listing, accepting, posting, treating, teaching — passes through these five gates in order. The gate that rejects a request names itself, so the user always sees an exact path to remediation.

GATE 1
PSS
Is this person who they claim to be, at the confidence level this action requires?
GATE 2
CRB
Are their credentials currently fresh, or has a refill cycle expired?
GATE 3
JPS
Are they listed as performing this role on this platform, with the right skills?
GATE 4
Service
Has the sub-platform's own business logic accepted the request?
GATE 5
DMO
Are there any open disputes, bans, or freezes against them?

SQ Level — the universal trust score

SQ0Unverified
View-only across all platforms.
SQ1Basic Verified
Email + OTP confirmed. Can buy, browse, view jobs.
SQ2Compliance Verified
CNIC + NADRA match. List small-ticket items, take part-time gigs.
SQ3Financially Verified
Bank/IBAN validated. Take payments, withdraw to bank.
SQ5Professional Verified
Business reg + physical franchise visit. Run a store, hire staff.
SQ7Expert Verified
Regulator-licensed (PMDC / ICAP / HEC etc). Operate as a licensed professional.
SQ10Elite Certified
Multi-year track record + audited. Run a franchise, certify others.
